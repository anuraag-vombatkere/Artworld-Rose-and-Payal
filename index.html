Our Story

Rose & Payal

Born from a shared obsession with beauty and craft, Artworld was founded by Rose and Payal — two women who believed that the spaces we inhabit should inspire us as deeply as the art we hang in them.

From hand-picking artisans across Asia to styling luxury events across three continents, we bring the same curatorial eye to every project — whether it's a 500-person gala or a single perfect vase for your living room mantle.

We are based in Singapore, and we work globally.
